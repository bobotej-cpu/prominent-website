About

De-Prominent Hotel and Suites offers comfort, luxury, and excellent hospitality with modern rooms, delicious meals, and top-class services. Located in the heart of Abeokuta, we provide an unforgettable stay with personalized attention and world-class amenities.

mission

At prominent hotel and suites our mission is to provide an exceptional hospitality experience thats exceeds the expectation of our guests through personalized service, luxurious accomodations and atmosphere thats creates lasting memories by combing local charm with modern amenities, fostering a culture of warmth, inclusivity and promoting sustainability.

Vision

Our vision is to become a premier destination where guests feels at home enriched by our genuine service, sustainability practices and deep connection to hospitality.

Our Restaurant
